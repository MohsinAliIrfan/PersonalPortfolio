About Me

My name is Mohsin Irfan and I’m a Computer Science student at the University of Westminster and I love programming. It’s always been my dream to work in the tech industry, and I’m finally making that dream a reality.

I grew up in Lahore, and when I was younger, I was always fascinated by computers. My parents bought me my first computer when I was eight years old, and I remember spending hours on it, playing games and exploring every corner of the internet. From then on, I knew that I wanted to work with computers when I grew up.

I studied hard at school and got good grades, which led me to university. Nowadays, I spend most of my time coding or learning about new technologies. In my spare time (when I have any!), I like to play video games, cricket or listen to music.

I’m passionate about my chosen career path and determined to succeed. The tech industry is constantly changing and evolving, and there’s always something new to learn – which is what makes it so exciting! In the future, I hope to work as a software engineer or developer for a major tech company.
